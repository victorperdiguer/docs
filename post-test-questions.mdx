---
title: "Post-test Questions"
---

Post-test questions are asked after a tester finishes the flow. They are optional, but useful for capturing insights that navigation alone won't tell you, or specific feedback on topics that matter to you.

## Adding questions

Type one question at a time and press **Enter** to add it. Each question can be up to 200 characters, and you can add up to 10. The helper button on the top right of the field will help you with ideas on how to frame the questions.

## What to ask

Post-test questions work best when they surface reflection the flow itself can't:

- "Was there any point where you weren't sure what to do next?"
- "Did you miss any information on the product details page?"
- "Did you find the product price appealing?"

Keep them open where you want texture, and specific where you're chasing a known concern. You'll read the answers back alongside the rest of your results in [Post-Test Questions](/post-test-questions-1) under Results.
