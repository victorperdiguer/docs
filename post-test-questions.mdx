title: "Post-test Questions"
---

**Post-test questions** are asked _after_ a tester finishes the flow. They're optional, but useful for capturing what navigation alone won't tell you — perceived difficulty, unmet expectations, or specific pain points in the tester's own words.

## Adding questions

Type one question at a time and press **Enter** to add it. Each question can be up to 200 characters, and you can add up to 10. The **Rewrite Question** helper will tighten any of them for you.

## What to ask

Post-test questions work best when they surface reflection the flow itself can't:

- "How confident were you that you completed the task correctly?"
- "Was there any point where you weren't sure what to do next?"
- "What, if anything, would have made this easier?"

Keep them open where you want texture, and specific where you're chasing a known concern. You'll read the answers back alongside the rest of your results in [Post-Test Questions](/post-test-questions-1) under Results.
