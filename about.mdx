The AI User Test is an unmoderated usability test run with [synthetic testers](/concepts/ai-participants) created in Uxia. Each tester navigates the flow on their own, thinks aloud as they go, and surfaces the confusion, friction, and dead-ends real users would hit in minutes, with no recruiting or scheduling.

You can start from almost any source:

- **Live URLs**: public sites, staging builds, or gated products. See [Source](/source).
- **Figma prototypes**: paste a shareable prototype link made with Figma, or use the [Figma integration](/integrations/figma) to import your frames and build the prototype inside Uxia.
- **Coded prototypes**: anything with a public URL, including Lovable, Base44, Cursor, Google Stitch or Claude. We also offer our [own MCP](/integrations/mcp) to connect Uxia to your agents.
- **Uploaded media**: bring your own images, designs or a video and build the flow yourself on Uxia's [media canvas](/media-canvas).

Whatever the source, every run gives you [a full results report](/results) on the experience your testers went through as they navigated.
