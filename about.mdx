The AI User Test is an unmoderated usability test — but instead of recruiting real participants, you run it with [synthetic testers](/concepts/ai-participants) created in Uxia. Each tester navigates your flows on their own, thinks aloud as they go, and surfaces the confusion, friction, and dead-ends real users would hit — in minutes, with no recruiting or scheduling.

You can start from almost any source:

- **Live URLs** — public sites, staging builds, or gated products. See [Source](/source).
- **Figma prototypes** — paste a shareable prototype link, or use the [Figma integration](/integrations/figma) to import and edit it inside Uxia.
- **Coded prototypes** — anything with a public URL, including Lovable, Base44, Cursor, Google Stitch, and Claude.
- **Uploaded media** — bring your own images or a video and build the flow yourself on Uxia's [media canvas](/media-canvas).

Whatever the source, every run gives you think-aloud [transcripts and an insights report](/results) on the experience your testers went through as they navigated.
