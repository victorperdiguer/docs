title: "Human Insights"
---

AI testing gives you fast, scalable signal — but you can go deeper by putting real people through the same flow. Any AI User Test can be extended into a [Human Test](/testing/human-test): real participants follow the same [mission](/mission) and [scenario](/test-scenario), and their results land in the same platform, so you can compare synthetic and human findings side by side.

## When to reach for human insights

This is the polish-then-validate pattern from [Use Cases](/use-cases) in practice. Run the AI test first to catch the obvious problems cheaply, ship the fixes, then bring in humans to confirm the findings and probe the subtler, higher-value questions that need a real person's judgment.

## Reading them together

Because both sets of results live in one place, you're not reconciling two separate studies. Where AI and human findings agree, you have a confident result. Where they diverge, you've found something worth understanding — a place your synthetic audience and your real users part ways, which is often a signal about the audience profile itself.
