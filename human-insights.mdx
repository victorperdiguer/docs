The **Human Insights** tab closes the research loop. From an AI User Test, it lets you generate a shareable link that carries the exact same setup and configuration your AI testers ran — the same mission, scenario, and flow — and share it with your own human participants in a **bring-your-own-testers** mode. Their sessions come back into this tab, so you can validate the AI findings against real people and get a full hybrid methodology in one place.

## How it works

1. Open **Human Insights** on a completed AI User Test.
2. Generate the shareable link — it replicates the AI test's setup exactly, so humans complete the identical task.
3. Share it with your testers and collect their sessions.
4. Review the results here and compare them against the synthetic findings to confirm what holds up.

See [Human Test](/testing/human-test) for the full reference on the human-testing capability.

## Overview

Once sessions come in, the tab opens with a metrics summary for the collected human runs:

| Metric | What it shows |
| --- | --- |
| **Total testers** | How many human sessions were collected |
| **Success rate** | Share that reached the goal |
| **Drop-off rate** | Share that left before completing |
| **Misclick rate** | How often clicks didn't move the session forward |
| **Average duration** | Typical time spent per session |

## Statuses

Each session carries a status so you can triage what's worth reviewing:

| Status | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| **Completed** | The tester finished the flow |
| **Abandoned** | The tester left before completing |
| **Missing questions** | The session finished but some post-test questions went unanswered |
| **Recording failed** | The session couldn't be captured |
