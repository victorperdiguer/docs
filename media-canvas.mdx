title: "Media canvas"
---

The **media canvas** is where you build a test flow from your own media instead of pointing testers at a live URL. Upload your screens or a recorded walkthrough, arrange them, and define how testers move between them — Uxia turns that into a navigable prototype the AI testers can explore.

## When to use it

Reach for the media canvas when you're testing:

- Static design screens exported from Figma, Sketch, or any design tool (JPG, PNG, or PDF)
- An image set representing a flow that isn't built yet
- A recorded video walkthrough of a product or concept

It's the counterpart to a [live source](/source): the same test, the same missions and testers, but you supply and wire up the screens yourself rather than having Uxia navigate a live site.

## Building the flow

On the canvas, you lay out your screens and connect them into the path you want testers to follow — defining which screen leads to the next so testers' actions move them through the flow the way a real interface would. Once the flow is set, the rest of the builder — [mission](/mission), [scenario](/test-scenario), audience, and [advanced settings](/advanced-settings) — works exactly as it does for a live test.
