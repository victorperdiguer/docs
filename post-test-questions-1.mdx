If you added [post-test questions](/post-test-questions) when building the test, this tab presents the answers to those open-ended follow-ups — grouped and summarized so you can read expectations and motivation, not just observed behavior.

## How answers are shown

Each question appears as an expandable accordion. Open one and you get a visual breakdown of the answers, the main themes grouped with percentages, and the individual tester quotes behind each theme.

For a question like "What do you value most in transport tickets when visiting a city?", Uxia might group the responses like this:

| Theme | Share of responses |
| --- | --- |
| Clarity and simplicity | 60% |
| International payment and language support | 40% |

<Note>
  The themes and percentages above are an example — yours are generated from your testers' actual answers, with each theme expandable to the underlying quotes.
</Note>

Because it distills open-ended responses into themes while keeping the raw quotes one click away, this tab is where you learn what testers expected and cared about, rather than only what they did.
