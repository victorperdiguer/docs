## About the Company

Uxia is operated by **UXIA TESTING SOLUTIONS, S.L.**, a company incorporated in Spain. Uxia is certified by ENISA (Spain's national innovation agency) under Spain's Startup Law (Law 28/2022), recognizing it as an innovative technology company subject to the regulatory framework established for emerging tech businesses in the European Union.

If you have security-related questions or need to report a concern, reach out directly at [hello@uxia.app](mailto:hello@uxia.app).
