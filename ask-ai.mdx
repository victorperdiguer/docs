---
title: "Ask AI"
---

Once your results are in, **Ask AI** lets you interrogate them in plain language instead of combing through every transcript by hand. Ask what the biggest friction points were, why testers dropped off at a particular step, or how a specific segment of your audience fared — and get an answer drawn from the session's transcripts and insights.

It's most useful for the follow-up questions a static report can't anticipate:

- "Which testers mentioned pricing, and what did they say?"
- "Summarize the confusion around the checkout step."
- "What did low-tech-literacy testers struggle with most?"
