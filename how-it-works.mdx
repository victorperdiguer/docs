---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Go to [https://platform.uxia.app/](https://platform.uxia.app/) and sign up / log in using your email address or a Google account.

    Once you’re in, you’ll land on the Uxia Home where you will see the Projects, Tests and Audiences you create.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a test type">
    Select the test type that matches your current goal. Each type is optimised for a different research goal:

    | Test type | Best for |
    | :-- | :-- |
    | [**AI User Test**](https://uxia-4bba6eee-admin-mcp-accessibility-guide-1a042c7.mintlify.site/testing/ai-user-test) | Validating designs, prototypes or URLs with task-based testing |
    | [**AI User Research**](https://uxia-4bba6eee-admin-mcp-accessibility-guide-1a042c7.mintlify.site/testing/ai-user-test) | Survey you audience to uncover needs and validate concepts |
    | [**Accessibility Test**](https://uxia-4bba6eee-admin-mcp-accessibility-guide-1a042c7.mintlify.site/testing/ai-user-test) | Validate the Accessibility of your designs using the WCAG 2.2 |
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up your test">
    Each test type has its own configuration options. Follow the guide for your selected test to learn how to set it up correctly before launching:

    - [**AI User Test**](https://uxia-4bba6eee-admin-mcp-accessibility-guide-1a042c7.mintlify.site/testing/ai-user-test)
    - [**AI User Research**](https://uxia-4bba6eee-admin-mcp-accessibility-guide-1a042c7.mintlify.site/testing/ai-user-test)
    - [**Accessibility Test**](https://uxia-4bba6eee-admin-mcp-accessibility-guide-1a042c7.mintlify.site/testing/ai-user-test)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Define your audience">
    Tell Uxia who should test your product. Uxia uses this information to generate AI synthetic testers that represent your target audience.Use the Audience Builder to create your audience and select the participants who will take part in the test.Every audience is built using up to three layers:

    1. **Sociodemographic Filters** – Segment your audience using more than 13 demographic and behavioral filters, such as age, gender, education, income, occupation, and more.
    2. **Psychological Profiles** – Select participants based on personality traits and behavioral characteristics to better match your target users.
    3. **Audience Enrichment** _(Custom Plans only)_ – Enhance your audience with insights from your own research, customer data, or internal documentation to create more representative synthetic testers.

    **Shortcut:** Paste your product URL into the Audience Builder, and Uxia will automatically suggest an audience profile based on your product and industry. You can use the suggested audience as-is or customize it before saving.Once an audience is created, it can be re-used in any test.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch and wait">
    Review your configuration one final time, confirm the number of testers, and click **Launch Test**.

    Uxia’s synthetic testers begin their sessions immediately, and the Test Transcripts tab will start to populate so that you can begin analysing results from the moment you launch the test. Unlike traditional user research, there is no recruitment delay, no no-shows, and no scheduling coordination.

    You’ll receive an email notification when your report is fully ready.
  </Step>
</Steps>
