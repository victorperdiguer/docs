Audience Enrichment is a custom service that builds synthetic testers directly from your own data. Instead of relying on a representative default panel, you feed Uxia the audience knowledge you already have — personas, ICP definitions, interviews, research — and it generates testers that reflect _your_ users, so the feedback maps directly onto your product and business.

## When to use it

Enrichment pays off most when your user persona or ICP is already well mapped — when you understand your users' pains, needs, goals, and end-to-end experience in detail. A B2B tool for compliance officers at mid-market financial firms has a very different user than a consumer fitness app; when your audience has distinct workflows, vocabulary, risk tolerances, or trust patterns, a generic panel can miss the nuances that matter most. Enrichment closes that gap and makes the resulting feedback far more relevant.

## What you can provide

Uxia accepts a range of inputs, and the more specific and detailed they are, the more precisely it can configure your testers:

- **Long-form interviews** — user interview recordings or transcripts
- **Persona definitions** — narrative or structured personas, including through one of our approved templates
- **ICP definitions** — firmographic and psychographic descriptions of your ideal customer
- **Audience segments** — breakdowns by behavior, lifecycle stage, or use case
- **Behavioral and research data** — anonymized usage patterns, onboarding analytics, support-ticket themes, or past survey and usability findings

Enrichment can also layer in extra context so testers reason more like your real users — for example, **knowledge of your competitors** or **previous versions of your product**, so feedback accounts for what your audience is already familiar with.

## How Uxia uses your data

Uxia doesn't simply quote your documents back as tester behavior. Your inputs are combined with Uxia's behavioral frameworks and public demographic data to produce profiles that are both grounded in your audience reality and behaviorally coherent.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Share your audience data">
    Add your documents — interviews, personas, ICP definitions, segment data, or research outputs. Uxia accepts common document formats.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Uxia generates candidate profiles">
    The enrichment engine analyzes your inputs and produces synthetic tester profiles tailored to the audience you've described — each with demographics, behavioral patterns, and psychographic traits derived from your data.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and select your testers">
    You see the generated profiles before anything runs. Inspect each one, confirm it fits your intent, and hand-pick the lineup for your session. You're never locked into an auto-assigned panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch your test">
    Your enriched testers run the session, and the resulting transcripts and insights reflect how your actual target audience would experience your product.
  </Step>
</Steps>
