title: "Frame Upload"
---

The **Upload Frames** step is where you add the screens or recordings you want Uxia to audit.

## What to upload

Drag and drop your files into the upload zone, or click to browse. Supported formats:

- **Images** — PNG, JPEG, WEBP
- **Videos** — MP4, MOV, AVI

You can upload up to **10 files**, at up to **100 MB** each.

<Tip>
  Upload representative screens from the real flow rather than isolated mockups without context. Use images for static screen checks, and reach for video only when interaction behavior is what you need evaluated.
</Tip>

<Note>
  You need at least one file to continue. If you click **Next** with nothing uploaded, Uxia shows "Please upload at least one file."
</Note>

## Review and launch

Once your files are attached, continue to the **Summary** step to review your setup — conformance level and uploaded frames — and start the audit.
